McCausland College of Arts and Sciences

Discover more choices and more opportunities to build essential knowledge and skills for unlimited career paths. We prepare students to approach modern challenges with curiosity and lead with purpose in an ever-changing world. Through broad academics, groundbreaking research and real-world experience, our students and faculty drive progress in South Carolina and beyond.
